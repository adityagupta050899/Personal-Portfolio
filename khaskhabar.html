Generative AI for AML Compliance

Organization: Agricultural Bank of China

Tools Used: Python, R, Llama 3.3, RAG, MySQL

Designed and implemented a Generative AI model to automate the filing of Suspicious Activity Reports (SAR) in alignment with Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations. The project involved integrating financial data pipelines using MySQL and Jupyter, optimizing RAG model accuracy, and reducing hallucination risks by fine-tuning LLMs.
